Bodoni

STILE BODONI

The Bodoni style is one of the most known styles between the XVIII and XIX centuries. It is a neoclassical style, in which there is a lot of attention for the beauty and the readability of words. Wide spaces and curate, but never eccessive serifs. Only a few images, symbols or ornaments are used, since the attention has to be on the text, on the beauty of the text.

FONT AND MEASURES
